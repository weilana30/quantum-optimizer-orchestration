#Defines how optimizers are allowed to operate to ensure consistency

Input:
    Logical quantum circuit

Allowed Operations:
    - Gate cancellation
    - Gate merging
    - Commutation-based rewrites
    - Template matching
    - Peephole optimization
    - Block collection
    - Synthesis
    - Resynthesis
    - Logical qubit permutation
    - Rebase to common basis Synthesis

Disallowed Operations:
    - Layout selection
    - Physical qubit assignment
    - Coupling-map-aware optimization
    - Routing
    - SWAP insertion
    - Scheduling
    - Noise-aware compilation
    - Calibration-aware compilation
    - Pulse-level optimization
    - Backend-specific Synthesis
    - Hardware-specific gate substitutions
    
Output:
    - Logical quantum circuit

Metrics:
    - 2Q gate count 
    - Depth
    - Runtime

Basis:
    basis_gates=["id", "rz", "sx", "x", "cx"]
